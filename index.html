How to Buy

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.app.

Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $FLOKI. If you dont have any SOL, you can buy SOL from an exchange or cross chain swap and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium, Jupiter..

Connect to Raydium or Jupiter. Go raydium.io or jup.ag in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $FLOKI token address into Raydium or Jupiter and confirm the swap. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
